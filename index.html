PPL UMS

Introduction

Welcome to PPL UMS (The App) developed by Scube Technologies Ltd. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for and use PPL UMS, we collect the following information:

  • Name: Your full name for account identification
  • Email Address: Used for account authentication, communication, and password recovery
  • Phone Number: Contact information for account verification and support
  • Password: Encrypted credentials for secure account access
  • Company Name: Your organization's name
  • Company Address: Your organization's physical address

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • Login Activity: Date and time of login sessions
  • Utility Data: Electricity consumption, water usage, steam usage, and other utility metrics
  • Cost Calculations: Energy cost data and consumption patterns
  • Analytics Data: Usage patterns, feature interactions, and app performance metrics

Downloaded Content

When you use the download feature in PPL UMS:

  • Downloaded Reports: We generate and allow you to download utility monitoring reports, charts, and analytics
  • Local Storage: Downloaded files are stored locally on your device and in your Photos library (with your permission)
  • Download History: We may track which reports you download for improving user experience
  • File Access: The app requires permission to save files to your device storage and photo library

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (iOS version, device model)
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

How We Use Your Information

Primary Functions

  • Account Management: Create and manage your user account
  • Authentication: Verify your identity when you log in
  • Utility Tracking: Calculate and display electricity, water, steam, and other utility costs
  • Analytics & Reporting: Generate graphs, charts, and tables showing your consumption patterns
  • Real-time Monitoring: Provide runtime energy cost and consumption data
  • Cost Analysis: Calculate and analyze utility expenses
  • Report Generation: Create downloadable reports, documents, and analytics for your records
  • File Management: Save downloaded reports to your device and photo library for easy access and sharing

Communication

  • Send important account notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Share updates about the App
  • Send password reset emails
  • Notify you about available report downloads

Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Optimize performance and user experience
  • Develop new functionalities
  • Enhance report generation and download capabilities

Permissions We Request

Photo Library Access

We request access to your photo library to:

  • Save downloaded reports and documents for easy backup and sharing
  • Allow you to select and attach images or documents from your library
  • Export utility reports in image or PDF format to your Photos

You control this permission: You can grant or deny photo library access in your device Settings at any time. Denying access will not affect other app functionality, but you won't be able to save reports to Photos.

Document and File Access

We request access to your device storage to:

  • Save monitoring reports, charts, and analytics documents
  • Allow you to open and view downloaded files
  • Enable file sharing with other apps

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Password Encryption: All passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms
  • Secure Storage: Email addresses and passwords are stored securely on encrypted servers
  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmission between the app and servers uses secure protocols
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic reviews of our security practices
  • Local File Protection: Downloaded files on your device are protected by iOS security features

Data Retention

  • Account information is retained while your account is active
  • You may request account deletion at any time
  • Utility data and analytics are retained to provide historical tracking and reports
  • Backup data is maintained for disaster recovery purposes
  • Downloaded files remain on your device until you manually delete them
  • Download history may be retained for service improvement

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access your personal information through your account settings
  • Update Information: Modify your name, email, phone number, company details, and address
  • Delete Account: Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Manage Downloads: Delete downloaded files from your device at any time
  • Control Permissions: Manage photo library and file access permissions in iOS Settings

Privacy Preferences

  • Email Preferences: Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Data Sharing: Control how your data is used for analytics (where applicable)
  • Download History: Request deletion of your download history
